Call for Abstracts

Present your research at DECO 2025! We invite participants to show their work with short presentations or posters. This is a great opportunity to share your findings and connect with colleagues.

Interested? Fill out our Call for Abstracts form before August 31st.

Note: in order to present at the workshop, you need to be registered for the workshops at ICDL 2025.

Topics of Interest

The workshop will cover a wide range of topics, including but not limited to:

  • Cognitive development and learning
  • Developmental psychology,
  • Developmental robotics and AI,
  • Virtual embodiments,
  • Sensorimotor control,
  • Active perception and learning,
  • Open-ended learning and intrinsic motivations,
  • And more...
